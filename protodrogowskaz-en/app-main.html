A letter from your child

Dear mom and dad!

It's me Hania, your Crumb, Crumb. I have been with you for a long time. And this is the first letter I am writing to you. Mommy, you carried me under your heart while Daddy was stroking your belly, where I was safely waiting to meet you and my Siblings.

And did you know, Daddy, that you stroked me then, too? Now I am already here, I have parents and siblings with whom I would so much like to be in our home already. However, I know that I'm still here in the hospital, and all in all I'll tell you that it's cool I have a lot of colleagues here, nice ladies nurses come to poke me on the tummy recently one called me a strawberry yet I don't know what it means, I thought it must be something cool.

There are also days when I don't know what's going on because I'm sleeping soundly, the doctor comes in with big eyebrows and talks, and then they keep poking me with something. It's not fun, but maybe it will get me with you sooner. I can feel Mommy worrying and grieving, you know..., just because I've already come out of my belly doesn't mean I don't know how you feel.

I feel you worry and stress about me a lot when I don't gain weight. You know what? I would like to ask you something. I'm asking you not to feel sorry for me when you are searching in your mind for things you could have done differently because maybe they would have helped and I wouldn't be lying here, or maybe not, who knows?

Remember that I have an instinct that helps me deal with what is difficult. All I need is for you to master your fears a little, because it will be difficult for me to grow with them. Sometimes you have to take risks, be brave. Aaaaand ask a lot, in this I intend to be a master, because even with my eyes, legs, hands, tongue I can ask. I liked it here in the World, I'm staying! I love you and look forward to when we will already be together. I don't need anything else, just your closeness and love. Maybe there is a difficult road ahead of us, not always straight, full of interesting turns. Feels that what will lead us is love and hugging, Lots of hugging. Remember : I LOVE YOU!

Hugs Dear Friends,
Your Hania, Crumb of Hope

What is the Incubator...
A parent's glossary
What is an Incubator (OHIO)?
You certainly know from movies or television those special beds for the smallest babies. They are actually incubators!
Scroll down


Reproduces the conditions under your heart
The task of the incubators is to try to recreate, at least minimally, the conditions that prevailed under your heart.

Monitors the child's life
It is a device equipped with elements that monitor the baby's vital signs and those that support its vital functions, if necessary.

Baby likes the touch
As much as possible, touch your baby as often as possible. You won't be able to take him in your arms at first, but he can feel you and sense your tenderness and care.

This is extremely important. Throughout this difficult time, you can become a support for him and a moment of respite from medical procedures.

What does OHIO stand for?
Not sure but Ms. Jola will fill in!
